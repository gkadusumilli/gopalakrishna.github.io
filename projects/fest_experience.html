Zero1 Fest - Around money & wealth

  • Month of travel: April 2024
  • Location: Jayamahal palace, Bengaluru
  • Iternary: 1 day

Zero1 by Zerodha is a financial literacy platform that educates people on money, finance, and wealth creation. They host podcasts featuring respected figures in the field. On April 28th, they organized a festival featuring veteran financiers and founders to discuss their experiences in startups, wealth creation, and management. I received an invitation to the festival at Jayamahal Palace after registering.

Here is my experience of spending a day on the weekend at Zero1 Fest.

Leaders & Artists invited to the fest [VIDEO]

The Zero1 team deserves a huge shoutout for pulling off a fantastic festival! They managed to seamlessly host over 1600 attendees and keep a packed schedule running smoothly from 11:30 AM to 8:45 PM. Incredibly, most events started on time, with only minor 15-minute deviations. This level of organization for such a large-scale event is truly impressive!

Health - Panel with Nas Daily (CEO of NASDAILY), Nithin Kamath (Founder of Zerodha), JC (Founder of Fittr), Shashank (Founder of Whole Truth Foods)

                           
                   
  • Discussed the importance of health, fitness to earn money & build a company
    •                
  • An individual should always care for the food that is being taken, which defines the personality of the physical body. (Home cooked, whole food, Protein rich)
    •                
  • 3 Metrics to look by an individual to determine health & fitness - Resting heart rate, Vo2Max, lean body mass
    •                
  • Discussed how Indian food majorly lacks protein and how this gap can be leveraged by building companies around food-enriching proteins

Making your 1st crore - Panel with Karthik Rangappa (Content head - Zerodha Varsity), Abid (CEO of Sensibull)

  • Karthik suggested early investment in the career and rightful investment in mutual funds & stocks for a long term.
  • Abid being an investment banker & trader emphasized more on taking risks & waiting for the right moments.
